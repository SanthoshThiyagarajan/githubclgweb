BSc(COMPUTER SCIENCE)

BSc Computer Science deals with the theoretical knowledge and skills in the field of computer science and its applications in the real world for various uses. BCA deals with the development of software and computer applications. Programming and networking are the core areas of the subject.

BCA

. BCA or Bachelor of Computer Applications is an undergraduate course that will build the knowledge of the students regarding computer language. By completing this three-year course, the students will be able to build their careers in the field of Information Technology.

BSc(IT)

BSc Information Technology is an undergraduate science course of 3 years dealing with software, databases, and networking designed to impart theoretical and practical knowledge on storing, processing and managing information securely.

BA(English)

What is BA English? Bachelor of Arts in English Literature is an undergraduate course with a duration of three years. This course emphasises the detailed study of English Literature which includes poetry, proses, essays and several other literary pieces.

BBA

BBA or Bachelors of Business Administration is an undergraduate degree course offered to students after Class 12. For students who wish to build a career in the management field, BBA is a good option to opt for. Admission to BBA programme can be given on the basis of merit or entrance tests. Earlier, only a few private schools and universities provided this course; however, due to the high demand for the course and its promise, nearly every major institution in India now provides BBA. As a result, it is now a lot more accessible. Companies actively seek BBA graduates and offer excellent pay and benefits. Also Read: Why BBA is considered the best choice after 12th?

BSc(AI&ML)

There is a wide-ranging scope in developing machines in expert systems, speech recognition, robotics, computer vision, language detection and more. On the basis of factors like robotics, automation and sophisticated use of computer software, one can excel in the AI domain

BCOM

The full form B Com is B.Com. It is a 3-year course that revolves around banking, finance, and accounting principles. Should I choose B Com or B Com (honours) after the 12th? Both the courses are good, but it depends upon you whether you want to specialize in a particular domain and doB Com Honoursor you want to have an overview of all the subjects in the field of commerce and do plain B Com. The industry demand for B Com honors is comparatively higher than the general B Com.

MCA

MCA course provides in-depth and extensive knowledge about various aspects of computer applications. Students are taught everything related to software, hardware, and networking with the studies of IT, Data structures & Mathematics. MSc Computer Science provides core competence in various aspects of Computer Science.

MBA

A Master of Busines Administration (MBA; also Master in Business Administration) is a postgraduate degree focused on business administration.[1] The core courses in an MBA program cover various areas of business administration such as accounting, applied statistics, human resources, business communication, business ethics, business law, strategic management, business strategy, finance, managerial economics, in a manner most relevant to management analysis and strategy.

MSW

Social work focuses on improving the standard and well-being of human life, with importance to individuals who are oppressed, in poverty, vulnerable, or unheard. Individuals aspiring to promote social change and justice can pursue Masters in Social Work (MSW) programs.

MCOM

M.Com helps students launch careers in the quickly growing corporate sector. Specializing in commerce courses is advantageous for those aiming for careers in business and management. The prospects open to those with an M.Com degree are expanding.
